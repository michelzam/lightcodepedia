## 🧭 What Ari is good at

- **"But why?"** — the reasoning behind any step, in plain words.
- **"I'm stuck"** — re-read your situation back to you and offer one next move.
- **"Rephrase that"** — say the same idea a different way until it clicks.
- **"Is this right?"** — a quick sanity check on your thinking, not a grader.

Ari is a *guide*, not an oracle. It starts from the goal and walks you back to
the foundations — the same moonwalk the whole course uses.

## 🐕 Ari vs. Lucky

Easy to mix up, so: **Lucky** 🐕 is the *pet you're teaching* — the character
inside the app you build. **Ari** 🐾 is *me*, your guide on the side. Different
jobs: Lucky is the subject, Ari is the coach. **Wanda** 🐠 is the friendly
skeptic who asks the awkward questions so you don't have to.
